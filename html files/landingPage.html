About Us

We are a community-driven initiative aimed at promoting urban farming in Masajja Division. Our mission is to provide accessible resources and education to individuals and families looking to grow their own food.

Join us in our effort to build a sustainable food system!

Our Services

Garden Planning

We provide personalized garden planning services to help you optimize your growing space and maximize your yield.

Organic Fertilizer

We offer a range of organic fertilizers to help you nourish your soil and promote healthy plant growth.

Workshops

We host regular workshops on a variety of topics, including composting, seed starting, and pest management.
